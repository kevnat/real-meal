Privacy Policy for Real Meal

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Real Meal ("the App") is operated by Kevin Nathan ("we", "us", "our").

What We Collect

  • Your email address, used only to create and manage your account.
  • Meal plans, recipes, and shopping lists you enter in the App. This data is stored on our servers so it can be restored if you reinstall or switch devices.
  • Basic usage events (e.g. "meal plan created") sent to PostHog, a third-party analytics service. These events are linked to your account but are used only to improve the App.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect advertising identifiers, device identifiers for advertising, precise location, contacts, health data, financial information, or any data for third-party advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To back up and restore your meal data across devices.
  • To improve App features through aggregated usage analytics.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase servers in the United States. AI chat messages are processed by Groq and are not stored after the response is returned.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at kevin.max.nathan@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days.
